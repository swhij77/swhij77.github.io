A CONCEPT APP DESIGNED TO SOLVE SOCIAL WELFAIR-RELATED PROBLEMS.

FUBAO - 福報

ABOUT FUBAO - 福報

Fubao is a concept app designed for people who is in need and for prople who want to be a part of making some changes. Our goal is to matching these people more efficiently in order to help more pelple who have always been forgotten by the society.


OPPORTUNITY

According to Executive Yuan of Taiwan, charity donation in 2013 is estimated as 537 billion NTD (53,700,000,000). It is safe to describe Taiwanese poeple are very generous when it comes to helping other people in difficult times. However, charity scandals are often reported from time to time these years. Where does the donation go? How does the donation help people? Are the resources controled by "major" charity organization? These question started to catch our attention. As a contributor, I wanted to come up with a new solution to improve this situation.

Understanding our users and painpoints

How to make the minority group visible in public eyes and how to make the resources distribute more properly is what I considered at first as I began the project. I wanted to understand how charity works under current system, hence I made the appointment with employees at Rare Disease Organization and Shin-Lu Social Welfare Foundation to get a better perspective of social worker’s point of view. I found out their daily work is very detailed and very time consuming.

Next, I interviewed with people who has contributed in the past. They usually have their own cause and purpose to participate in charity. At the same time, they tend to have doubt in charity organization. Why people want to help others that they don’t know is also a crucial lead in my research.

With this in mind, I designed an app specifically for potential contributors to ease their doubt and make them willing to come back. Throughout the process, I learned the difficulties in designing platform since there is always two sides of target audience that need to be considered. Furthermore, how to create a trust machinism is also what I struggle the most in the design process.


The design process

IDEA

This project not only tries to solve contributor sides’s issue, but also concerns the people who are unable to reach out for help. We find out what concerns the contributor from trusting their good deeds will ended up in the right place. From there, I designed a machinism to show the contributor the transparency of the cash flow.

Sketch

I began to sketch as many different directions as I could, pick the best one solution. After getting to a good place with each iteration in Axure, I would then turn to Sketch to build user interface.

wireframe

First, I sketch rough wireframe that based on iOS 7 guideline. And I think that sketching is a great way to get the ball rolling and can save hours before our start the digital design.

Flow and UI design

Second, I start plan specific interaction flow so that I can figure out my interaction process and start design UI.

Design - Telling stories

Everyone has a story, through telling the story, I want the contributor to understand the difficulties and delimmas of the receiver's. So they can contribute not just based on pity, but based on what they believe.

Design - Trusting

Every project is proposed by individual or charity organization with solid purpose and cause, Fubao will response for examing the project then announce it.

Design - Sharing

In every project, contributor is not just giving their money. They will know exactly what their money is used for.
